About Me

Being a traditional realism artist, I love creating visuals and I feel this CISC 1600 class has been perfect so far in helping me realize the creative aspects of programming. Imagine rock climbing the mountains of Yosemite National Park or feeling the rush and freedom while skiing down a snow covered mountain.
